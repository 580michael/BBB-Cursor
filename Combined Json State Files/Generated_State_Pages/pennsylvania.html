What are the typical bail bond fees in Pennsylvania?
Bail bond premiums in Pennsylvania are generally standardized at 10% of the total bail amount, as established by state regulations. This fee is non-refundable and represents the bondsman's service charge. For instance, a $5,000 bail would require a $500 premium payment.
